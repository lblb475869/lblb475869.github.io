什么是现代东方医学（MOM）

核心定义：MOM 为一套以东方哲学的方法论为底层、以“模型（Model）”为基本单元的独立医学体系。

What is Modern Oriental Medicine (MOM)?

Core Definition: MOM is an independent medical system grounded in the methodology of Eastern philosophy, using “models” as its basic unit.

框架结构

学科定位与目标

定位为独立学科；明确研究对象、目标与评价标准。

方法论与规范

假设—求证—复现；标准化与一致性；记录与审计要求。

模型库（Model Registry）

以模型为基本单元；命名、版本、最小模型集；新增/迭代/淘汰准则。

证据与确权（Evidence & DOI）

材料公开路径；DOI/时间戳；复核与更正版流程。

应用与边界

诊断/输出表达格式；与现代医学协作边界与安全原则。

Framework

Discipline & Goals

Positioned as an independent discipline; defines objects, goals and evaluation criteria.

Methodology & Standards

Hypothesis → verification → reproducibility; standardization and consistency; recording and audit.

Model Registry

Model as the basic unit; naming, versioning, minimal model set; rules for adding, iterating, retiring.

Evidence & DOI

Open materials workflow; DOI/timestamp; re-review and revision process.

Applications & Scope

Format of diagnostic/output expressions; collaboration boundary and safety with modern medicine.
